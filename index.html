Mental Health Resources for Healthcare Workers

Welcome to the world's first collaborative database of mental health resources for dealing with a pandemic, made by healthcare workers for healthcare workers.

We know COVID-19 is taking its toll on your mental health. We're here to help. To use this database, please click on the category of resources you're interested in. Alternatively, you can also search by clicking on a relevant tag or resource type at the bottom of this page.

If you wish to contribute a resource, please click the circle with a plus sign at the very bottom of the page.
